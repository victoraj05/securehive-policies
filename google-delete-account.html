Deleting your SecureNest account will permanently remove all your data, including passwords, notes, and categories. This action cannot be reversed.

Due to our zero-knowledge architecture, we cannot recover your data once deleted. Please make sure to export any important information before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Through the SecureNest App

1

Open SecureNest App

Launch the SecureNest app on your device and sign in to your account.

2

Navigate to Settings

Go to your profile/settings section by tapping on your profile icon or the settings menu.

3

Find Delete Account Option

Scroll down to the "Security & Recovery" section and tap on "Delete Account" (displayed with a red warning).

4

Confirm Your Identity

Enter your master password or use biometric authentication to confirm your identity.

5

Final Confirmation

Read the warning message carefully and type "DELETE" to confirm you want to permanently delete your account.

What Gets Deleted

Data Permanently Removed:

  • All Passwords: Every password entry stored in your vault
  • Secure Notes: All notes and sensitive information
  • Categories: Custom categories and organization settings
  • Account Information: Email address, user ID, and account metadata
  • Preferences: App settings, sync preferences, and biometric settings
  • Recovery Data: Recovery codes and backup information
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and session data

Data Retention:

  • No Personal Data Retained: Due to our zero-knowledge architecture, we cannot access your encrypted data
  • Anonymized Analytics: Some anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement (contains no personal information)
  • Legal Compliance: Only if required by law and only for the minimum period necessary

Deletion Timeline

Immediate: Account access is revoked and login is disabled
Within 24 hours: Account marked for deletion and removed from active systems
Within 7 days: Grace period ends (for accidental deletions) - data permanently deleted
Within 30 days: All account data completely removed from our systems
Within 90 days: Data removed from encrypted backups and disaster recovery systems
